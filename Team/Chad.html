Team Focus Areas

How the team divides design, fabrication, controls, and presentation work across the project.

Mechanical Design and Assembly

Andre Garcia and Dominick Dekker lead mechanical design, assembly planning, machining, and build execution for the robot hardware.

Systems, Analysis, and Prototyping

Zahid Padilla leads system architecture and prototyping, and supports design analysis. Alexa Drab supports simulation, animation, media, and design visualization.

Software, Electrical, and Strategy

Chad Senecal leads control systems, software, electrical systems, and technical strategy for the full robotic platform.
